# Database

This public repository does not expose the private database schema of NexoLocal.

At a high level, the platform stores data across the following business domains:

- identity and access control
- companies, memberships, and subscriptions
- customers, suppliers, products, and sales
- finance, cash movements, and business reporting
- support workflows
- tax-oriented records and compliance-related operations

The detailed table structure, field design, relationships, indexes, and internal persistence rules are intentionally kept private.
